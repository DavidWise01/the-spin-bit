> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **SCIENTIFIC**.

SPIN · THE ORIGINAL BIT — the electron's one true binary degree of freedom, up or down. An interactive STERN-GERLACH experiment: a beam of silver atoms streams from an oven through an inhomogeneous magnet and splits into EXACTLY TWO spots on the screen — not the continuous smear classical physics predicted. Toggle classical vs quantum to see what should have happened and didn't; live ↑/↓ counters. THE THESIS: two states, nothing between = the definition of a BIT. Spin is the ×2 that turns the orbital ladder (1·3·5·7·9) into the capacities (2·6·10·14·18), the 4th number in an electron's [[the-four-addresses]] address, and the ONE place the atom↔computing analogy IS the physics (a real qubit's home), not a costume. Stern-Gerlach is real (1922, two dots on a plate). Two-layer honest: the sim is schematic; spin-½ being genuinely two-valued is exact. The weld point of the whole [[the-atomic-byte]] cluster.
